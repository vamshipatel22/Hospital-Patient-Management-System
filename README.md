# Hospital-Patient-Management-System
# Problem Statement
The Hospital Management System (HMS) is a software application designed to streamline, automate, and centralize the day-to-day operations of a hospital. It enables efficient handling of patient information, appointments, medical records, staff details, billing, and administrative workflows. The system reduces manual paperwork, minimizes human errors, and enhances the overall efficiency of healthcare delivery
# Objectives
To automate routine hospital operations.
To maintain digital records of patients, doctors, appointments, and treatments.
To improve accuracy of patient data and reduce paperwork.
To provide quick access to patient history for better diagnosis.
To support hospital staff with tools for managing schedules and resources.
To ensure secure storage of sensitive healthcare data.
# Features
1. Patient Registration & Management
Add, update, view, and delete patient records.
Store personal, medical, and contact information
Maintain patient medical history and diagnosis
Search patients by name, ID, or mobile
2. Doctor & Staff Management
Add and manage doctor profiles
Specialization-wise listing
Doctor availability and schedule management
Assign patients to doctors
3. Appointment Scheduling
Book, reschedule, and cancel appointments
Check doctor availability
View patient and appointment history
Avoids overlapping appointments
4. Inpatient / Ward Management
Manage room and bed allocation
Track admitted patients
Update treatment notes and progress
Shift or discharge patients
5. Billing & Invoice Generation
Auto-generate bills based on services
Include doctor charges, pharmacy, lab tests
Download/print invoices
Payment status tracking
6. Medical Records Management
Electronic medical records (EMR) storage
Upload lab reports, prescriptions, notes
Quick access for doctors during diagnosis
7. Pharmacy & Lab Module (optional)
Track medicine inventory
Add/update lab test reports
Manage stock-in/out
8. Reports & Analytics
Patient reports
Appointment statistics
Revenue report.
Doctor performance summary
9. Authentication & Access Control
Admin and staff login
Role-based access (Admin/Doctor/Staff)
Secure data management
10. Responsive User Interface
Easy-to-use dashboard
Mobile-friendly design
Clean navigation for fast workflow.
